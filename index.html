About the College

Who We Are

Through an innovative choice of University Foundation Studies and Diploma/Advanced Diploma programmes, the College provides pathways to university. Small class sizes and personalised academic attention provide a supportive environment for students to flourish in their tertiary studies. Our relationship with The Miscatonic University allows us to provide high-quality education and resources to our students.This is why students from all around the world choose The College and The Miscatonic University as their top educational options in Australia.




What We Do...

Liberal Arts & Communication

With an online communication or liberal arts degree, you can gain highly transferrable skills and knowledge.

Nevermore College's liberal arts programmes offer a well-rounded education that may be easily customised with a range of minors. Improve your communication abilities; broaden your worldview through cultural studies, history, and psychology; or address contemporary challenges through social and political science. A liberal arts degree or certificate online can help you build essential abilities for success in any career.

Sorcery & Applied Magical Arts

Immerse yourself in the ancient art of witchcraft and earn an advanced diploma or a degree in applied magical arts.

The empirical reality confronts professionals with limitations deemed impossible to overcome. Education & Teaching

Earn an online education and teaching graduate degree or certificate from Nevermore College, a distance education leader.
